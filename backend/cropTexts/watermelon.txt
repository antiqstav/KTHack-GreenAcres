Watermelon
Watermelon is a refreshing, juicy fruit, best grown in warm climates with sandy, well-drained soil.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 1 inch deep and space plants 2-3 feet apart.
Maintain a row spacing of 6-8 feet for sprawling vines.
Best practices:

Use compost or manure to enrich soil fertility.
Provide consistent watering and avoid overwatering near harvest.
Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
