Coffee
Coffee is a globally loved beverage crop that grows best in shaded, fertile, and well-drained soil in tropical regions.

How to plant it:

Use seeds or saplings from healthy coffee plants.
Plant in a shaded area with fertile, well-drained soil.
Space plants 5-8 feet apart in rows 8-10 feet apart.
Best practices:

Maintain soil pH between 5.0 and 6.5.
Use mulching to retain soil moisture.
Prune plants to improve air circulation and encourage flowering.
