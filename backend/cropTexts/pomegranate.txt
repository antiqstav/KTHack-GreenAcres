Pomegranate
Pomegranate is a hardy fruit tree known for its juicy, antioxidant-rich seeds, thriving in semi-arid to subtropical climates.

How to plant it:

Plant saplings in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
Space trees 10-15 feet apart for growth.
Best practices:

Fertilize with a balanced mix of NPK.
Water moderately and increase during fruiting.
Prune annually to remove dead or overcrowded branches.
