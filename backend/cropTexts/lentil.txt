Lentils are a staple legume, rich in protein and fiber, grown in cooler climates and well-drained soil.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 1-2 inches deep and space rows 6-8 inches apart.
Plant in sandy or loamy soil during late winter or early spring.
Best practices:

Inoculate seeds with rhizobium to enhance nitrogen fixation.
Avoid planting in waterlogged areas.
Weed regularly to prevent competition.
