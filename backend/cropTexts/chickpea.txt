Chickpea
Chickpeas are a staple legume, rich in protein and grown primarily in arid and semi-arid regions.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 2-3 inches deep in rows spaced 18-24 inches apart.
Plant in well-drained loamy soil during early spring or fall.
Best practices:

Rotate crops to prevent soil depletion.
Avoid overwatering to reduce the risk of fungal diseases.
Apply phosphorus-rich fertilizers.
