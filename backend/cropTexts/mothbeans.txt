Moth Beans
Moth beans are a drought-resistant legume, cultivated for their protein-rich seeds, thriving in arid and semi-arid regions.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 1-2 inches deep and 3-4 inches apart.
Maintain a row spacing of 12-15 inches.
Best practices:

Use well-drained sandy or loamy soil.
Apply phosphorus-based fertilizers at sowing.
Monitor for pests like pod borers and adopt crop rotation to maintain soil health.
