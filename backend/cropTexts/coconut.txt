Coconut
Coconuts are versatile tropical crops that thrive in sandy soil and coastal regions with good sunlight.

How to plant it:

Use healthy coconut seedlings or nuts.
Dig a hole about 3x3 feet wide and deep.
Space trees 20-25 feet apart.
Best practices:

Ensure adequate sunlight and salt tolerance in soil.
Water young palms regularly, especially during dry spells.
Apply organic manure and protect from pests like rhinoceros beetles.
