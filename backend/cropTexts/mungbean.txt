Mung Beans
Mung beans are a protein-rich legume crop, valued for their edible seeds and sprouts, flourishing in warm climates.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 1-2 inches deep and 3-4 inches apart.
Keep rows spaced 12-18 inches for better airflow.
Best practices:

Use light, sandy-loam soil for optimal drainage.
Apply balanced fertilizers with phosphorus and potassium.
Irrigate moderately and avoid waterlogging.
