Banana
Bananas are tropical fruits known for their fast growth and rich potassium content, thriving in warm, humid climates.

How to plant it:

Use suckers or rhizomes from mature banana plants.
Plant in pits 2 feet deep, spaced 10-15 feet apart.
Best practices:

Water frequently but avoid waterlogging.
Apply organic manure and potassium-rich fertilizers.
Protect plants from strong winds to prevent damage.
