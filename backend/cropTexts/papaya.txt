Papaya
Papaya is a fast-growing tropical fruit tree known for its sweet, nutritious fruits, thriving in warm, sunny regions.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds or plant saplings in a well-drained spot with full sun.
Space plants 6-10 feet apart to ensure adequate growth.
Best practices:

Use rich, organic soil and apply nitrogen fertilizers monthly.
Water regularly but avoid waterlogging.
Protect from frost and strong winds.
