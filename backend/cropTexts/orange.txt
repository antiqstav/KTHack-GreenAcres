Orange
Orange trees produce juicy, citrus fruits and grow best in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

How to plant it:

Plant saplings in holes twice as wide as the root ball.
Space trees 15-20 feet apart for good air circulation.
Best practices:

Fertilize with a balanced NPK mix during the growing season.
Water deeply but infrequently.
Protect against citrus pests like aphids and scale insects.
