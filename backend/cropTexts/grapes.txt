Grapes
Grapes are a popular fruit crop grown for fresh consumption, wine production, and raisins, thriving in sunny climates.

How to plant it:

Use grafted vines or cuttings from healthy grape plants.
Plant in holes 8-10 inches deep and space vines 6-10 feet apart.
Best practices:

Train vines on trellises for better growth.
Prune annually to promote fruiting.
Ensure good drainage and avoid overwatering.
