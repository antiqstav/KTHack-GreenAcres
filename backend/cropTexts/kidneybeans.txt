Kidney Beans
Kidney beans are protein-rich legumes, commonly grown for their culinary uses and easy cultivation in well-drained soil.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds 1-2 inches deep and space them 4-6 inches apart in rows.
Plant in loose, well-drained soil in early spring.
Best practices:

Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
Use trellises for support if growing pole beans.
Fertilize with phosphorus-rich soil enhancers.
