Jute
Jute is a natural fiber crop widely used in making textiles, ropes, and bags, requiring moist, fertile soil.

How to plant it:

Sow seeds directly into moist, fertile soil.
Space rows 6-8 inches apart and sow seeds about 1 inch deep.
Best practices:

Plant during the rainy season for optimal growth.
Weed regularly during early growth stages.
Avoid waterlogging, which can stunt growth.
